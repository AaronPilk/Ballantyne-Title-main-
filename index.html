About

Ballantyne Title Company

Ballantyne Title was built on excellent customer service, impressive turn times, and extensive knowledge of the industry. Headquartered in Charlotte, NC, we service North Carolina and South Carolina from the mountains to the coast.

With years of expertise, we have built a reputation for delivering accurate and reliable title reports, resolving complex title issues, and offering personalized customer service. We understand the importance of timely and efficient closings, and we strive to exceed our clients' expectations every step of the way.

Our dedication to customer service, attention to detail, and use of cutting-edge technology are what set us apart. We take pride in our reputation, and we look forward to earning your trust and your business.
