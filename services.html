Commercial Title

Commercial title insurance for offices, retail, industrial and mixed-use.

Commercial transactions move fast and have more moving parts. We coordinate with attorneys, lenders and investors to deliver clean commitments and hold deadlines — even when surveys, easements or chain-of-title issues complicate the picture.

Common situations we handle:

Closing Services

The right attorney, notary and partner for every closing.

At Ballantyne Title, we understand that finding the right attorney or notary for your closing can be a crucial step in the real-estate process. That's why we've established partnerships with a network of experienced professionals spanning various regions.

Whether you need legal expertise to navigate complex issues or require a notary to authenticate documents, our team can connect you with the right professionals to ensure a smooth and efficient closing experience. We also offer remote closing services, providing flexibility and convenience for clients who may not be able to attend in person.

We prioritize customer satisfaction and strive to provide comprehensive support throughout every stage of your real-estate transaction.

Consumers

More than just title insurance.

Ballantyne Title offers more than just title insurance — we are your one-stop shop for all your real-estate needs. Whether you're paying cash or getting a mortgage, we can help you find the right closing attorney, realtor and mortgage broker to ensure a smooth transaction.

Title insurance can be confusing, but it's a crucial part of the real-estate process. It protects you from potential ownership disputes and other title issues that could arise after you purchase a property. Without title insurance, you could be at risk of losing your investment or facing costly legal battles.

Don't let uncertainty jeopardize your property ownership. Let Ballantyne Title provide you with the peace of mind that comes with knowing your investment is protected.
